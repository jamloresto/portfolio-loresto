CEAT Centennial Logo

Winner of the University of the Philippines - Los Baños College of Engineering and Agro-Industrial Technology's Centennial Logo Design Contest last October 2011.

The gear represents engineering and technology as a whole. When the teeth of the gear are omitted, the structure inside of the gear shows a steering wheel representing continuous drive. The oblation represents excellence. The number 1, in 100 is an arrow directing upwards symbolizing improvement. Maroon and green are the colors of the university which molds every person involved in CEAT-UPLB. Golden yellow represents aspiration towards a brighter future.

Tools used:
  • Adobe Photoshop
