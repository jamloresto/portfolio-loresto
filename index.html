Jessa Mae Loresto

Front End Developer · Video Editor · Chemical Engineer

Jam is a registered Chemical Engineer who has a background in Process Engineering, Quality Control and Quality Assurance. She also has a background in Human Resources specializing in General Administration, Talent Acquisition, and Learning and Development. Her adept skills in various computer programs and fascination in designing has led in her decision to become a Web Developer.

She initially worked with My Own Staff as a Web Designer. She has done a number of Wordpress sites catering US-based clients. She is currently working as a Front-end Developer, mainly using React, working on projects from Australian-based and other global clients.

CEAT Centennial logo

Winner of the University of the Philippines - Los Baños College of Engineering and Agro-Industrial Technology's Centennial Logo Design Contest last October 2011.

The gear represents engineering and technology as a whole. When the teeth of the gear are omitted, the structure inside of the gear shows a steering wheel representing continuous drive. The oblation represents excellence. The number 1, in 100 is an arrow directing upwards symbolizing improvement. Maroon and green are the colors of the university which molds every person involved in CEAT-UPLB. Golden yellow represents aspiration towards a brighter future.

Tools used:
  • Adobe Photoshop
